    Introdution on Shankar Event Planner




    Shankar Event Planner is a company that offers high-quality services to help plan your business event in Nepal. Their team of experts provides different types of support for your meetings and special events. As a team, we manage everything from the venue to accommodation while arranging meetings, wedding ceremony, birthday party, bartabanda, anniversary party, conferences, exhibitions etc. Additionally, we can obtain the best prices and excellent services for you because of our generous experience, understanding of the market, competence, and feeling for our customers. You can count on us to deliver the best solutions for any kind of event, customized or themed events even for last-minute demands.

    As mentioned above, Shankar Event Planner’s team consists of a group of brilliant event planners who are doers and visionaries working tirelessly to get the most out of the event industry. In fact, Shankar Event Planner was established with the aim to serve potential customers from various fields. Being one of the inexpensive and beautiful countries with access to all kinds of world-class services, we choose to grow our business in Nepal.


    As per services, we aim to offer the best assistance to our clients. Some of them are the following:


    • Event Concept and Design
    • Vendor Coordination, Selection and Management
    • Planning, Coordination, Marketing and Promotion
    • Guest Services, Post-Event Services, Technology and AV Services
    • Types of Events Catered - Corporate, Social, Charity, Public & Private Events

    As a service-providing company, we value our client’s responses. Positive client experience generates a recurrent effect and encourages our work. Customer satisfaction serves as Shankar Event’s primary business objective and its primary source of inspiration.
